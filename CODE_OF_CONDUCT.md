# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Assume good intent.
- Focus on technical discussion.
- Avoid harassment, abusive language, and personal attacks.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Threatening or violent language.
- Harassment based on identity or background.
- Deliberate disruption of project activities.
- Posting harmful or discriminatory content.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to the maintainers through the repository issue tracker or the documented security contact.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, block users, or close contributions that do not follow this code of conduct.
